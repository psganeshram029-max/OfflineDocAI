ℹ️ About OfflineDoc AI

OfflineDoc AI is an intelligent, offline-first medical assistant designed to deliver essential healthcare support without relying on internet connectivity. It enables users to enter patient details, analyze symptoms, and receive immediate guidance — making it highly effective in rural areas, emergency situations, and low-network environments. The system integrates key features such as accurate symptom-based analysis, real-time emergency alerts, secure patient history management, and downloadable medical reports in PDF format. By operating completely offline, OfflineDoc AI ensures reliability, speed, and accessibility when timely medical assistance is most critical. Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare by providing a dependable solution that works anytime, anywhere — regardless of connectivity constraints.”**
